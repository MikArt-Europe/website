# How to use the shop?

To use the shop, type `/shop` in the chat. This will open the shop GUI, where you can buy and sell items.

## Buying items

To buy an item, click on the item you want to buy. This will open a confirmation dialog.
Click on the + or - to select how much you want to buy and then click on the confirm button to buy the item.

## Selling items

To sell an item, right-click on the item you want to sell. This will open a confirmation dialog.
You can select how much you want to sell and then click on the confirm button to sell the item.
